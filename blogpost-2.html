5 WAYS TO MAKE YOUR WEBSITE MODERN

We share our best ideas in our blog
Home / Blog / Blogpost

Your business card on the web

in today's society a modern and responsive website is more important than ever. It’s on the website that customers form their first impression of a company or organization. If you are met by a slow and ugly website you quickly go to the competitor instead. The trends change quickly so your site can get outdated in just a few years. Therefore, it’s important to keep up with the latest trends and keep your site up to date. This is even more important if you want to display trust and authority. So today I thought I might share with you my top 5 tips on keeping your site fresh and modern.

Page speed

The speed of the page is probably the most important factor when making a website. There have been studies that show that 90% of visitors leave after 3 sec if the page is not loading. Therefore, you must plan the content of your page so that the right amount of information is displayed. Although the page loads fast for you it might be slow for someone with poor internet. To ensure that the page is fast enough, you can use tools such as Google PageSpeed insights. to test your website's loading times. Enter your domain and click analyze the tool will compare your site across a multitude of variables and give you a rating between 1-100. You also get suggestions for changes you can apply on the website to increase speed. The most common recommendation is to compress images.

Optimize for mobile phones

50% of all browsing of the internet is now made on the phone. Is your website not optimized for this traffic you can lose a lot of customers. To make sure your site is suitable for mobile traffic you can examine your page with Google Developer Tools. Click F12 when you are on your site and select the icon that looks like an iPad and a phone at the top left of the module window. With this tool, you can examine how your website looks on different screen sizes. You can also use the Google Pagespeed insights that I linked above to see how fast your site loads on the average phone.

Great images

One of the best ways to modernize your website is to use big beautiful images. Good pictures give a modern look and makes the content on the page easier to consume. You therefore want to focus on finding images associated with your content and insert them with regular intervals. One of my favorite places to find free images online is Unsplash.com . Here you can pick and choose from thousands of high resolution images in the public domain. Remember to compress the images before you upload them to your website.

Structure

Before you start frantically uploading amazing images and cool product descriptions, it is important to think through the site's structure. How do you want your visitors to navigate the content on the page. How do you create a natural flow from the landing page to conversion. If you think this through and plan out a natural path that leads your visitors to the right information in the right order, your sales will increase significantly. Therefore you should take plenty of time to think about what information your visitors want and in what order. Then after you have sketched out a plan for the optimal structure and you have a clear picture of how you want your visitors to navigate the page, you can get on with the fun part of building the page.

Design trends

it is important to keep up with the latest trends. The more similar your website is to others, the easier it will be for your visitors to navigate your site and get the right impression of your business. Is your website too experimental, it can be difficult for your visitors to find what they're looking for. Not to say that you shouldn’t experiment with interesting ideas just know that it might affect the ease of use. I recommend that you do some research on other similar pages and their appearance. In certain niches this is not enough, since everyone in the industry has old and outdated pages. Then you can study niches similar to yours and find inspiration there.
